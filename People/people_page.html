Melissa Custer

Melissa Custer graduated from Loma Linda University with a MPH in Environmental and Occupational Health and has been a Registered Environmental Health Specialist since 2013. She began working at Loma Linda University in March 2011, first with Dr. Steve Yellon, researching cervical remodeling in preterm birth. She joined the Wilson Lab in 2013 and is now primarily responsible for immunohistochemistry and the general oversight of the lab.

Nathan Kern

Nathan is a computational biologist working for Dr. Sean Wilson at Loma Linda University. He applies computational techniques to the study calcium signaling in pulmonary smooth muscle cells.

He previously worked for Dr. Wonpil Im at the University of Kansas, where he studied the behavior of LLO inside membrane bilayers and published in the Biophysical Journal.
